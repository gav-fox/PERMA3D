June 2026 - Mapping the environment

The most important dataset in PERMA3D is the site survey.

A survey grade 3D topography is the foundation everything is built on.

I am starting with what i have which is an iPhone 14, spirit level and a printer. 

Using the PIX4D with printed ground control points (GCP) I will scan my first test site (my garden) to try to create an accurate 3D model of my garden. 

The main challenge at this stage is getting axcuracy without RTK relying on spirit level and handheld measurements to get an accurate x,y,z plot.
